Here is a stopwatch, programmed to help get you through intensive work. When you hit the start button, block out all distractions, and apply yourself to whatever it is you want to achieve within 3 hours. There are three work periods, split into 70, 50, and 30 minutes. We allocated them as such to make you feel like you are going down a slide. By starting heavy and easing out, time feels like it is speeding up, and that rush makes staying focussed easier. Between work periods, 10 minute breaks provide a gap to reenergize and refresh. An alarm will ring when one starts, and again when one ends. We hope you have a productive session.

Disclaimer: However, if you don't, remember that the joys of life are not accrued through work alone. Spend time with your family and friends, and have fun; it fills life with meaning.
